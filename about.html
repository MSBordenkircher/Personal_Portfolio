About

Hi, I'm Michael Bordenkircher, this site is for me to show off some personal photography as well as some of my web design ideas. I have an Associates in Science from Jefferson Community and Technical College as well as enough credits from the University of Louisville for a minor in math. I currently instruct students in kindergarten to students in their senior year in high school at Mathnasium, and love my job. I am also an avid soccer fan, my favorite teams are Louisville City FC and Bayern Munich!
